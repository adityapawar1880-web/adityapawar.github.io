Proppa House Social Campaign

A multi‑platform campaign launching an eco‑friendly product range and inspiring sustainable living.

Overview

Proppa House challenged me to create excitement around their new line of sustainable home products. The campaign needed to raise awareness, position the brand as an eco‑conscious leader and drive both engagement and sales across a range of social channels.

Approach & Responsibilities

  • Researched target audience segments and crafted tailored messaging for each platform.
  • Developed a comprehensive content strategy and calendar covering Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn and X.
  • Produced original graphics and short‑form videos using Canva and Adobe Express, ensuring visual consistency and brand alignment.
  • Leveraged generative AI tools to ideate creative variations, accelerating production and enhancing content diversity.
  • Integrated targeted email campaigns via Mailchimp, aligning email content with social themes for cross‑channel cohesion.
  • Monitored performance through platform analytics, adjusting content in real time to maximise reach and engagement.

Results & Impact

  • Delivered a 30% increase in follower growth and a 25% uplift in overall engagement across social channels.
  • Generated over 1 000 sign‑ups to Proppa House’s newsletter and trial programme.
  • Improved conversion from awareness to purchase by 10%, contributing to a successful product launch.
  • Established Proppa House’s reputation as a forward‑thinking, eco‑friendly brand.

Through this project I honed my ability to orchestrate cross‑channel campaigns, align creative with strategy and deliver measurable outcomes. Feel free to explore the rest of my portfolio to see additional work.
