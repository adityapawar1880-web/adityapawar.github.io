AriSTa Helmet Safety Campaign

An awareness initiative encouraging riders and communities to prioritise helmet safety and save lives.

Overview

AriSTa’s mission is to promote road safety. This campaign aimed to elevate helmet awareness among commuters and families by combining engaging content with community outreach. As lead marketer, I was responsible for shaping the strategy, creative direction and execution.

Approach & Responsibilities

  • Conducted research into accident statistics and identified target demographics with low helmet usage.
  • Coordinated with local authorities and non‑profits to organise awareness events, including safety workshops and group rides.
  • Produced educational videos, infographics and social posts emphasising the benefits of wearing helmets.
  • Partnered with micro‑influencers and community leaders to amplify the message and foster trust.
  • Designed a dedicated landing page and promotional materials encouraging participants to pledge their support.
  • Engaged with followers via Q&As, quizzes and user‑generated content challenges.

Results & Impact

  • Achieved over 5 000 community interactions across social and event channels.
  • Contributed to a 20% increase in helmet adoption within the targeted demographic.
  • Received positive press coverage and recognition from local authorities for driving safety awareness.
  • Built partnerships that continue to promote road safety beyond the campaign’s duration.

This project allowed me to merge digital marketing with grassroots advocacy and reinforced the power of storytelling in changing behaviour. Browse my other projects to see more examples.
